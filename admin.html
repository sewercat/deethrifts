⚠️ localStorage limitation:
Drafts, live items, and customers are stored in your browser's localStorage only. Other visitors won't see released items unless you either:

1. Use "Export as Code" and paste the output into DEFAULT_PRODUCTS in cart.js, then re-upload to your host.
2. Replace localStorage with a real backend (Firebase, Supabase, etc.) for live updates across all visitors.

The customer database works per-browser — a customer recognized on their own device won't be recognized on a different device. For a true customer DB, you need server-side storage.
